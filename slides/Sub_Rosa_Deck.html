Sub Rosa · DemoThree outputs from one conversation
From a spoken consultation → a clean intake memo.
Pick a matter type, hit record, and talk. When you stop, Sub Rosa returns three things — and leads your eye to the one that matters most: what the client forgot to tell you.
① Facts Each fact paired with the verbatim quote from the transcript that backs it — so nothing is invented.
② Missing information The standard intake items the client never mentioned — a true set-difference against a checklist. The killer feature.
③ Follow-up prompts Short, neutral questions to ask next — never legal conclusions.

The LLM — the technical centerpiece — runs on the Hexagon NPU via ExecuTorch's QNN backend. CPU/XNNPACK is only the safety net. No INTERNET permission, OS-enforced.

Sub Rosa · RoadmapWhat comes next
→Future work
  • Whisper on the NPU. Move ASR to the Hexagon via QNN — both stages on the NPU.
  • Encrypted backup & export. A portable .subrosa file so a lost device doesn't mean lost notes.
  • Bigger & multilingual models. Larger decoders as device RAM allows; non-English intake.
  • More checklist packs. A growing, attorney-reviewed library across practice areas.
  • Long-consultation summarization. On-device running summary to beat the context window.
  • Local matter-management & iOS. Offline-first integrations and a second platform.
Today: the full product loop runs offline on the S25 — Whisper ASR, pyannote diarization, Qwen3-1.7B on the Hexagon NPU (~40 tok/s), the missing-info scorecard, encryption, and the live privacy proof.
